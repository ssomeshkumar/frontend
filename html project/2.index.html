ROCKSTAR

Anirudh Ravichander, commonly known as Anirudh, is a popular Indian music composer and singer in the Tamil and Telugu film industries. He was born on October 16, 1990, in Chennai, India. Anirudh gained widespread recognition and fame with his debut film soundtrack for the Tamil film "3" (2012), which included the viral hit song "Why This Kolaveri Di." Known for his youthful and energetic musical style, Anirudh has since worked on numerous successful films, delivering chart-topping songs across various genres. Some of his notable works include the soundtracks for movies like "Ethir Neechal," "Maan Karate," "Kaaki Sattai," and "Vedalam".

His collaboration with actor Dhanush has been particularly successful. Apart from film music, Anirudh has also composed independent singles and has ventured into the Telugu film industry, where he has created hit songs for movies like "Agnyaathavaasi" and "Jersey." With his innovative and catchy compositions, Anirudh has become a prominent figure in the Indian music scene, especially among the younger audience.
